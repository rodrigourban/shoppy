Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes, we ship our healthy food products to all regions across the country. Wherever you are, we've got you covered!

What is the shipping cost?

Shipping costs vary based on your location and the size of your order. You can see the shipping costs during the checkout process before finalizing your purchase.

How long does it take for my order to arrive?

The delivery time depends on your location and the shipping option you choose at checkout. Typically, orders arrive within 3-7 business days. For more precise estimates, please refer to our Shipping & Delivery page.

Are your products organic and non-GMO?

Yes, we are committed to offering organic and non-GMO products whenever possible. Look for the specific product details on each item's page to see its certifications and attributes.

Do you offer products for specific dietary preferences, such as gluten-free or vegan?

Absolutely! We offer a wide range of products tailored to various dietary needs, including gluten-free, vegan, and more. You can easily filter products by dietary preferences on our website.

Is my personal information safe when I shop with you?

Your privacy and security are our top priorities. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. You can learn more in our Privacy Policy.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various secure payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal, to make your shopping experience as convenient as possible.

What is your return policy?

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If you are not completely happy with your order, please refer to our Return Policy for detailed information on how to request a return or exchange.

What should I do if my order arrives damaged or incorrect?

Contact our customer support team as soon as possible, and we will be happy to assist you in resolving any issues with your order. Your satisfaction is our priority.

How can I get in touch with your customer support team?

We are here to help. You can contact our friendly customer support team via email at admin@shoppy.com. Our team is available to assist you with any inquiries or concerns.

Do you have a physical store where I can visit?

Currently, Shoppy operates exclusively online. This allows us to provide nationwide shipping and maintain competitive prices for our products.
